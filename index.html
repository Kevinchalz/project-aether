About Project Aether

Named after the forgotten fifth element — the invisible force that fills the space between stars — Project Aether exists where music meets the cosmos.

Born in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India, Kevin Charles grew up where rhythm runs deep. From the streets of South India to the clubs of St. Petersburg, Florida — his journey is written in basslines and beats. A Bachelor's in Computer Science from Coimbatore, a Master's in Cybersecurity in the United States — a mind built for systems, turned toward sound.

Trained at Beat Music Academy, Project Aether has performed at some of St. Pete's most celebrated venues — Red Thread, Jannus Live, Saigon Blonde, The Estate, and beyond. His sound moves across genres but lives in the space between: tech house, minimal house, open-format — always cinematic, always intentional.

Every Project Aether set is a journey — the dancefloor becomes a universe, and for those few hours, you float in the aether.
